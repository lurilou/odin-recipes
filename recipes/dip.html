Tahini Dip

This dip is just so delicious and refreshing. Add it to your salads, bowls, use it as a dip for fried things, eat it with potatoes...basically this goes with almost anything.

What you'll need:

  • Tahini (ths is the base of your dip so use roughly as much as you want to make)
  • ginger, grated
  • garlic, grated
  • lemon or lime (juice and zest)
  • maple syrup
  • soy sauce
  • optional: toasted sesame oil

Method:

  1. Add as much tahini as you want into a bowl.
  2. Add in grated ginger and garlic.
  3. Add soy sauce and syrup to taste
  4. Zest in some peel and add the juice of your lime/lemon and mix everything through. Taste and add more of the ingredients if needed.
